Title of Patent and filing Date.

Area of application / potential use

Novel Method of design and manufacture of a submersible hull with stiffener structure and its method of manufacture using additive manufacturing process
Filed 472/KOL/2015 on 28.04.2015

The patent covers two potential areas – one in underwater shell design that is useful for sensors, Autonomous Underwater Vehicle housing, submersible shell etc. and another as a metal additive manufacturing application.
